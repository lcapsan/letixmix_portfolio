DESCRIPTION

Development of the corporate identity and the book of animals for Wild Zoo. The brand Wild Zoo was born with the aim of representing the 'wild' at first sight. For this, use is made of the most common textures of wild animals: the snake, the cheetah and the zebra. As a book for children between the ages of 8 and 14, drawings have been made freehand, with lively and unusual colors, so that the book is suitable for all audiences, not just the children.

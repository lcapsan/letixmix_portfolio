YEAR

2017

WORK

Android APP

DESCRIPTION

Pantonera was an Android application designed as a Pantone color converter. It allowed users to enter a Pantone color code and instantly view its equivalents in RGB, CMYK, and hexadecimal formats. The app was created as a practical tool for designers, illustrators, and creatives working across digital and print media. It was previously available on Google Play but was later removed, likely due to trademark-related naming issues.
